# Stakeholders and Users

## Stakeholders
1. **Developers**
2. **QA (Quality Assurance) Engineers**
3. **Website Analyst**
4. **Website Users**

---

## Users
1. **Casual Storytelling Enjoyers**
2. **RPG (Role Playing Game) Players**
3. **FanFic Enjoyers**
4. **D&D (Dungeons and Dragons) Players**

---

## Reasons for Choosing Stakeholders and Users

### 1. Developers
- Responsible for frontend and backend ideation and implementation.  
- Ideation occurs after brainstorming on received responses from elicitation.  

### 2. QA (Quality Assurance) Engineers
- Test workflow of the website and overall performance.  
- Performance testing can be:
  - **Quantitative**: Time required to load a new page, time required to generate responses, etc.  
  - **Qualitative**: Coherence in story, relevance to prompt, etc.
