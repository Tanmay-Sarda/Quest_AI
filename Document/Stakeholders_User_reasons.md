## Reasons for Choosing Stakeholders and Users

### 1. Developers
- Role: Responsible for frontend and backend ideation and implementation.  
- Reason For Inclusion: Ideation occurs after brainstorming on received responses from elicitation.  

### 2. QA (Quality Assurance) Engineers
- Role: Test workflow of the website and overall performance.  
- Reason for Inclusion: Performance testing can be:
  - **Quantitative**: Time required to load a new page, time required to generate responses, etc.  
  - **Qualitative**: Coherence in story, relevance to prompt, etc.
